Biography

I hold dual Ph.D. degrees from the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences and City University of Hong Kong. Since July 2018, I have been part of the School of Information and the Beijing Academy of Artificial Intelligence at Beijing University of Technology. My research focuses on critical theoretical and applied technologies in artificial intelligence, particularly in computer vision and natural language processing. I have published numerous papers in esteemed international journals and conferences, including IEEE TIP, Pattern Recognition, Neurocomputing, ACM MM, COLING, and IEEE ICME. I have also led and contributed to several National Natural Science Foundation projects and received funding for high-level overseas returnees from Beijing in 2022.

My laboratory currently has several NVIDIA deep learning servers. We welcome students from relevant fields such as computer science and mathematics who are eager to engage in research. Preference will be given to those with a solid foundation in deep learning and strong programming skills. Please indicate your CET-6, IELTS, or TOEFL scores in your application. The lab is in a phase of rapid development, and we hope everyone can strive for progress together!
